[Up] = W
[Down] = S
[Left] = A
[Right] = D

[Attack] = U
[Jump] = I or Space
[Special] = O (not implemented)

----

* Press [Jump] again in mid-air to double jump
* Release [Jump] while jumping up to abort jump
* Hold [Down] + Press and hold [Jump] to dash and air-dash
* Release [Jump] mid-dash to cancel dash
