About myself
I grew up playing Contra, Ninja Gaiden, and Super Mario Bros on the Famicom, and have had a passion for video games for as long as I can remember. I decided to pursue a career in the video games industry when I enrolled at National University of Singapore.

I believe in creating nuanced, thoughtful games with real depth. I am most inspired by games like Monster Hunter, Invisible Inc, and Risk of Rain, games that ask much of the player but is incredibly rewarding in return. However, I also enjoy and appreciate light and casual "lunch break" games.

My preferred game development engine is Unreal Engine, and I also have experience working with Unity Engine. In a team, I contribute best as a mechanics designer, level designer, or programmer. I am also familiar with front-end web development.

I enjoy expressing myself through the arts. In my free time, I pursue photography, video, and dance. I also love to eat and can cook up a mean steak at the grill or on the stove.
